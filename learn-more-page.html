WHAT WE DO

TGG is not just another nonprofit

We believe intersectional grantmaking allows us to redefine what is possible in philanthropy.

Intersectionality is the idea that when it comes to thinking about how inequalities persist, categories like gender, race, and class are best understood as overlapping rather than isolated.

Our contributions to help support communities comes from grants that simultaneously address access to health, education, preparedness and more.

SHARE

Health Assurance

Our organization is dedicated to supporting children impacted by conflict, providing comprehensive care to mitigate the effects of war. We facilitate access to expert medical professionals, rehabilitation services, and psychosocial support, prioritizing their well-being and recovery.


Our mission fosters a brighter future for vulnerable youth, ensuring they receive the care they deserve for optimal healing. Through our efforts, we strive to restore hope, dignity, and opportunities for children affected by conflict, empowering them to thrive and reach their full potential.

Empowering Resilience

Providing essential resources like food, water, clothing, and shelter to vulnerable communities before natural disasters strike. This proactive approach helps families prepare, respond, and recover. By strengthening community infrastructure and supporting emergency preparedness, we can reduce the devastating impact of disasters.


Together, let's build a safer, more sustainable future for all, Collective action saves lives, and every contribution counts.
